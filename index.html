What is it?

About Zumbathon

Zumbathon for Cancer is a high-energy charity dance event that brings our community together to raise awareness and support for cancer research. Led by our teacher and powered by you, it's a day of non-stop Zumba, music, and good vibes — all for an incredible cause.

Every step you take, every song you dance to, makes a real difference in the fight against cancer.

FAQ

Do I need to know how to dance?

Not at all! Zumbathon is for everyone. Just show up, follow along, and have fun.

How do I register?

Click the Register button on this page to fill out our Google Form. It only takes a minute.

Is there a registration fee?

Details on registration and donations will be shared soon. Stay tuned!

What should I wear?

Comfortable athletic wear and sneakers. Wear pink if you have it to show your support!
