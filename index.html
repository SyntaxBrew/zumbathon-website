What is it?

About Zumbathon

A Zumbathon is a high-energy group fitness event that combines dance and exercise for a fun, community-driven workout. This event brings people together to raise funds and awareness for cancer research and support the BC Cancer Foundation.

Every step you take, every song you dance to, makes a real difference in the fight against cancer.
